AMADUDATU is a desert rat that runs on coffee and tacos.

AMADUDATU supports all cultural trends. From the lonliest crusty neo-raver to the much maligned skinhead hipster grandma juggalos. If you have a lifestyle we would like to support it, brand it, and run it into the gorund. AMADUDATU is a sleek high-functioning corporate capitalist enterprise with sound business practices masquerading as an edgy, socially-conscience, politically exciting, collective of artists and designers that meddle in web design, clothing manufacturing and gardening. AMADUDATU seeks the de-construction of the late-stage capitalist socio-economic paradigm which blemishes every aspect of human creativity and freedom. AMADUDATU hopes to lend a helping hand to those that seek to fundamentally alter any system which alienates people from themselves, their peers, or the fruits of their labor.
